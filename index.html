League Info

The league is open to all skill levels—whether you're brand new or competitive, there's a place for you. FAQ: KC Youth Spikeball League

Q: Who can join the league?

A: The league is open to middle and high school students in the Kansas City area. No prior experience with spikeball is required!

Q: How do I sign up?

A: Just head to the “Join” section of our site and follow the instructions. You'll get a link to our Discord, where everything happens.

Q: Do I need my own spikeball set?

A: Nope! We provide equipment at all league events. Just bring yourself, water, and good vibes.

Q: Is there a cost to join?

A: At the moment, the league is free to join. If that changes in the future, we’ll announce it in advance.

Q: Where can I ask more questions?

A: The Discord is a friendly environment for asking questions.
